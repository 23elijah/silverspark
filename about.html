Who We Are

Ettinger, a longtime jewelry maven, grew up in New York and recently relocated to Memphis to open the jewelry store of her dreams. She has worked as ajewelry designer and gem consultant for Michael C. Fina, Alyssa Fine Jewelry and Zales. She ha also worked as a freelance salesperson for Bergdorf Goodman in NYC where some of her clinets included celebrities seeking custom-made necklace and earring orders. Eileen is the daughter of James Louwden, founder of the national jewelry dtore chain, Louwden Diamonds. Her book, "Beyond the Yellow Diamond," about family jewelry heirlooms, will be available in stores next summer.

Daniel Olsen, COO

Olsen has worked as a designer for Karen Fitchwood designs for over 5 years where he personally crafted jewelry for clients all over the world. He spent 2 years in the U.S. Virgin Isles purchasing gems and stones for jewelry for clients stores all over the world. He currently has a popular design line on QVC where he specializes in charm bracelets and blue stones from the Caribbean. Daniel studied fashion and merchandising at the Rhode Island School of Design. Daniel is excited to collaborate with Eileen at Silver Spark and hopes customers from all over the world take advantage to Silver Spark's custom made offers.
